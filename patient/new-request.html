How it works

Your request goes directly to the selected pharmacy. They'll confirm availability and let you know when your medicines are ready for pickup.

Typical turnaround

Most pharmacies confirm within a few hours. If the medicine needs to be ordered, it typically arrives the same evening or next morning.

ShareCare does not guarantee medicine availability. Always confirm with your pharmacy before visiting.
