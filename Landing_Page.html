How it works

Built around lived experience

Share your story

Post about what worked for you — recipes, snacks, exercise routines, or how you handled a tough day. Your experience matters.

Community validation

Rate and discuss posts. The most helpful real-world strategies get surfaced by the community, for the community.

Safe and moderated

All content is peer-reviewed and reported. We keep this space respectful, honest, and free from harmful content.

Important: ShareCare is a peer support community only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
